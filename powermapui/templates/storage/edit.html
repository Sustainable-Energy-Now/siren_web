Field Guidelines

Duration: A "4-hour battery" means it can discharge at rated power for 4 hours. Energy Capacity = Power Capacity × Duration.

Round-Trip Efficiency: Should include all conversion losses. Typically 85-90% for Li-ion, 70-80% for pumped hydro.

SOC Constraints: Define operational limits. Many batteries reserve capacity (e.g., min=0.1, max=0.9).

Validation: Ensure charge/discharge efficiencies are consistent with round-trip efficiency.
