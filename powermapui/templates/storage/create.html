Field Guidelines

Duration: A "4-hour battery" means it can discharge at rated power for 4 hours. Energy Capacity = Power Capacity × Duration.

Round-Trip Efficiency: Typically 85-90% for lithium-ion, 70-80% for pumped hydro, 65-75% for flow batteries.

SOC Constraints: Many batteries reserve 10-20% capacity as unusable (min SOC = 0.1, max SOC = 0.9).

Cycle Life: Li-ion: ~5,000 cycles, Flow: 10,000+ cycles, Pumped hydro: 20,000+ cycles.
