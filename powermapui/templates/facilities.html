Facilities

This is a display of the defined Facilities. Facilities represent plant that comprise the grid infrastructure. Initial displays shows all defined facilities for all scenarios. You can choose a scenario from the drop down list to filter the list by the selected scenario. Every facility has a technology taxonomy. Where more than one defined technology is used in the same location as in a hybrid facility, they handled as a separate facility for each technology.
