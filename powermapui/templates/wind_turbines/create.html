Field Guidelines

Turbine Model: Use the exact manufacturer designation (e.g., "V90-2.0 MW", "GE 2.5-120").

Rated Power: Typically ranges from 500 kW to 15,000 kW for modern turbines.

Hub Height: Common values are 80m, 100m, 120m, 140m depending on turbine size.

Cut-in/Cut-out: Typical cut-in: 3-4 m/s, cut-out: 20-25 m/s.
