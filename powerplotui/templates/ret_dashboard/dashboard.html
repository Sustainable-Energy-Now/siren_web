Understanding Demand Measures

Operational Demand: Total grid-sent generation minus storage charging (BESS + Hydro). This represents electricity delivered through the grid.
Underlying Demand: Operational demand plus rooftop solar (DPV). This represents total electricity consumed including behind-the-meter generation.
